Last One Only Breadcrumbs

A breadcrumb solution that only exposes the last item in the trail by default, but exposes the entire trail when space becomes available.

Pros

  • Saves space by only exposing the last element in the breadcrumb trail.
  • Small screen users are still able to traverse the breadcrumb trail

Cons

  • Small screen users require a full-page refresh in order to go back multiple levels
  • Small screens still download the extra markup even though it's not used
