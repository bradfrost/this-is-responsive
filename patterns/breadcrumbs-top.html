Across the Top Breadcrumbs

A breadcrumb trail that simply wraps onto a new line.

Pros

  • Simple to implement. Flows as is.
  • Provides access to full breadcrumb trail.

Cons

  • Can occupy multiple lines, therefore obscuring the core page content.
  • Proximity taps may result when breadcrumbs occupy multiple lines.
